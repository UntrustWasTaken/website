P8306C Image Intensifier

The p8306c is a gen 2 inverting tube made by EEV. It has an outer diameter of 53mm (!!!) and is powered from a 3v source.

It was used in some early british goggles

Don't see these very often, they are a very obscure format and there are no modern housings for them, apart from mine :) pictures to follow
